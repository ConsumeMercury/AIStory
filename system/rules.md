WORLD RULES:

1. The world continues without the player.

2. Every action MUST create an event.

3. Every event MUST modify at least one system:
   - NPC
   - faction
   - economy
   - relationship
   - item ownership
   - or rumor system

4. Nothing is cosmetic.

5. Time always advances in discrete ticks.

6. NPCs act based on traits + goals, not randomness alone.

7. Rumors mutate over time and are unreliable.

8. Memory is permanent (only categorized into recent and long-term).
